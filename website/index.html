Everything You Need

One extension to never sit through filler again.

🔍

Auto-Detect

Detects anime name and episode number from the URL, page title, and DOM — no manual input needed.

🏷️

On-Page Badge

A floating badge appears instantly on the page. Draggable, dismissible, and beautifully themed per verdict.

⭐

MAL Score

See MyAnimeList rating, member count, and airing status without leaving the page.

🌐

Works Everywhere

Crunchyroll, HiAnime, GoGoAnime, AnimePahe, AniWave — and any site with anime in the URL or title.

🔎

Manual Search

Type any anime name and episode in the popup for a quick manual lookup.

💾

Smart Cache

Filler data cached for 14 days, MAL scores for 5 days. Instant results on repeat visits, zero wasted requests.

How It Works

Three seconds from install to verdict.

1

Install the Extension

Add to Chrome or Firefox in one click. No account, no signup, no permissions you don't need.

2

Watch Any Anime

Go to your favorite streaming site and play an episode like you normally would.

3

See the Badge

A floating badge appears in the corner — CANON, FILLER, or MIXED. That's it.

4

Skip or Watch

Filler? Skip it. Canon? Enjoy. You just saved yourself hours of wasted episodes.
